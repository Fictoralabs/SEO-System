Understand what type of content is ranking

Concept

After the site structure and page structure are created, the next important part is content.

Before writing content, do SERP analysis. SERP means Search Engine Result Page. The purpose is to understand what Google and GEO platforms want for a keyword.

Search the keyword in Google incognito and check the top results. Also check ChatGPT search for the same keyword. The result type shows whether the content should be a blog post, landing page, video, local listing, or another format shown in the search results.

Task steps

  • Search the keyword in incognito. Use the exact keyword, then study the first page of Google.
  • Check the top 7 results. Identify what dominates: listicle, local map, blog post, landing page, video, local pack, Google Business listing, or AI Overview.
  • Check GEO behavior. Use ChatGPT search to understand what AI search is surfacing for the same keyword.
  • Decide the content type. The dominant result type shows what kind of content should be created.
