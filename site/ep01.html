The episode

Three steps · zero guesswork

What you'll do

  • Find the real competitors. Search your primary keyword in incognito. Grab the top 8–10 organic results — not who you think your competitors are, who Google says they are.
  • Inventory every page. For each competitor, log every page they ship: home, services, locations, FAQ, blog, case studies, comparisons.
  • Draft your sitemap. Look for the pattern. Pages that show up across 7+ competitors? You probably need them too. Pages no one has? Skip or wait.
