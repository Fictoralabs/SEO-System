Task from the reel

Find the common homepage structure

Task steps

  • Open the same 10 competitors. Use the competitor list created in EP01.
  • Check each homepage. Study the exact homepage structure used by each competitor.
  • Track common sections. Look for hero section, about, service, FAQ, testimonial, why us section, contact form, and footer.
  • Find the repeated pattern. The common structure followed by most competitors becomes the structure to follow.
