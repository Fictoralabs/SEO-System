Find the common homepage structure

Concept

EP01 created the site structure. EP02 uses the same competitor list to create the page structure.

Open the exact 10 competitors collected in EP01 and check how each homepage is structured. A category homepage commonly includes sections like hero, about, service, FAQ, testimonial, why us, contact form, and footer.

The goal is not to copy one website. The goal is to find the common structure followed by competitors and use that pattern for the homepage.

Task steps

  • Open the same 10 competitors. Use the competitor list created in EP01.
  • Check each homepage. Study the exact homepage structure used by each competitor.
  • Track common sections. Look for hero section, about, service, FAQ, testimonial, why us section, contact form, and footer.
  • Find the repeated pattern. The common structure followed by most competitors becomes the structure to follow.

  • Mark sections that repeat across competitors.
  • Use the repeated pattern as the homepage structure.
